City West Office Park

33 - 35 Saunders Street, Pyrmont, NSW 2009, Australia
Commercial
328 - 1,480 sqm
Fund: GAIP

Centrally located on Saunders Street in Pyrmont's restaurant and entertainment precinct, City West Office Park offers high quality, modern office space with close proximity to a range of amenities and public transport options. The estate is occupied by a variety of leading brands including Network Ten, Nova 96.9 and APN Outdoor.

Highlights

  • High quality office space
  • Ample on and off-site amenities
  • Centrally located in the Pyrmont café and restaurant precinct
  • Easy access to Sydney CBD, Sydney Airport and City West Link
